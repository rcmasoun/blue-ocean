About

Since 2018Building stronger steel supply chains

Special Steel Supplier & Stockist in India

Blue Ocean Steels LLP is a trusted special steel supplier, stockist and dealer serving manufacturers, fabricators and engineering companies across India.

We ensure reliable access to alloy steel, round, hex, flat and bright bars through strong sourcing partnerships and a well-managed inventory system.

Our portfolio also includes billets, wire rods, steel scrap and customized steel components, supporting a wide range of industrial applications.

One Supply Chain Partner for Every Special Steel Challenge

Domestic Specialty Steel Mills

Concerned about:

  • MOQ mismatch with customer batch sizes
  • Staggered delivery demands
  • Longer customer credit cycles
  • Capacity utilization and cyclic demand

BOS Solution:

We aggregate demand, manage warehousing, lift committed material and help mills serve fragmented MSME buyers efficiently.

International Specialty Steel Mills

Concerned about:

  • Entering the Indian special steel market
  • Finding the right customers
  • Managing approvals, credit and local compliance
  • Building distribution across India

BOS Solution:

We act as your India supply chain and distribution partner with customer access, warehousing, compliance support and market development.

Domestic Customers

Concerned about:

  • Right quality steel at the right price
  • Small or exact quantity requirements
  • Regular availability of grades and sizes
  • New grade development and sample lots

BOS Solution:

International Customers

Concerned about:

  • Reliable sourcing from India

  • Flexible commercial models

OEMs
